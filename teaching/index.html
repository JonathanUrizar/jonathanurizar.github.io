Teaching

It is my firm belief that every student should be entitled to a quality education in music. If a student has the strong desire to learn and excel in music, he or she can attain any goal they set in mind. As an educator, I strive to teach a student to perform at a highly proficient technical level, but more importantly, I promote the overall effect of the work from a musical standpoint. Every note is the most important note you'll ever play.

Teaching Rates

$30/Half Hour lesson; $50/Hour

Location

Lessons take place at Kennesaw State University. In home lessons can be arranged for a reasonable additional fee on a case by case basis.

Expectations

Each student is expected to come to each lesson prepared and ready to perform. This means that the student is expected to practice on a daily basis to make lesson time as productive and efficient as possible.

Cancellations

A notification of a cancellation or rescheduling should be given at least 24 hours in advance of the lesson. Failure to do so within 24 hours notice will result in a $20 fee.
